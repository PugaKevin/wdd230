Color Run!


by Louis Lane

We are committed to excellence and to our communities, and we are proud to be part of the cities around Idaho. We are excited to share our passion with you and to work with you to help you live a better you. We have created this space to serve and inspire you. We are passionate about your community. We want you to enjoy the benefits of your work and the pride you bring to the community. We are passionate about what we do.

Last year we had our amazing Color Run. Basically you and a couple of friends will pay a small fee to enter the run. You will run around the track making friends and meeting new people! While walking, running, or even fast walking, by standers will throw powder with different colors on you.
