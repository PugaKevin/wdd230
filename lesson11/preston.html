A Farming Community


by Louis Lane

Our story is very simple and we have been fortunate to grow a family of farmers in this community. Our organic garden provides sustainable, organic, quality healthy food and we work hand in hand with our suppliers to ensure that the ingredients are sourced from the best available sources.

Our range of organic produce includes fresh herbs, vegetables, herbs and spices, dried fruits and nuts, and we have a range of other products including breads, jams and jellies, and bread mixes. We also supply the London borough of Croydon with a range of fresh produce.
