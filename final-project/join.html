Membership
Membership Info --Non-Profit ($100) – This membership program is a means of incentivizing, giving, and involvement within nonprofit organizations and associations. The organization extends additional engagement opportunities to members in exchange for donations.
--Bronze ($200)- This membership program gives the business an opportunity to be randomly selected to participate in upcoming events that will help your business stand out, as well as having help from other members on important upcoming events. You will also get a 24/7 phone number that you may call if you have any questions. Someone will get back to you in 24 hours with an answer to your question and/or additional resources.
--Silver ($400)- This membership program gives you an opportunity to be randomly selected as well, however you will have a better chance. You will have everything that Bronze has as well as advertisement on our website randomly throughout the year, as well as shout outs on TV stations, radio stations, and social media.
--Gold ($1000)- This is the one you want. You get everything from previous membership options as well as guaranteed spots on upcoming events, prime time advertisement on TV and radio, as well as being part of the few that get to vote on the upcoming events, as well as any other important issues that come up to the City of Washington. Your business is going to be heard. You also can mentor smaller businesses if you want to help. Your business will also have the opportunity to network with other businesses to help the community.
Regardless of your membership your business still matters to the City of Washington! We are here to serve you!
