The color scheme as you can tell is something easy and simple. We start with an light blue color background. The reason for this decision is because it makes it super easy to read black on it and white as well. This way I can make back banners(like on the menu) then add white to it to make it pop. Light blue is a type of blue that is very inviting and calming.
